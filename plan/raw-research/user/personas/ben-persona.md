# Persona: Marcus (Casual Mobile Player)

Age: 22  
Occupation: College Student  

Tech Environment: Mobile phone (iOS), plays on the go  

## Goal
Marcus wants a fast and simple slot machine experience where he can immediately start spinning without navigating menus. He prefers quick gameplay sessions and wants clear visual feedback for wins and losses.

## Behavior
Marcus plays in short bursts throughout the day (between classes, waiting, etc.). He does not spend time learning complex systems and prefers intuitive controls. If the game is confusing or slow, he will leave quickly.

## Needs
- One-click spin interaction  
- Fast reel animations (low delay)  
- Clear win/loss feedback  
- Simple UI with minimal clutter  

## Frustrations
- Slow spin animations or delays  
- Too many pop-ups or interruptions  
- Confusing layout or too many features  
- Not knowing if he won or lost immediately  

## Scenario
Marcus opens the app during a short break. He expects to tap “spin” instantly and see results within a second or two. If the game requires extra steps or feels slow, he exits and does not return.
