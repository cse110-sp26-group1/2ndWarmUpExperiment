Persona: PJ Morgan

Age: 40

Occupation: JP Morgan Investment Banker

Tech Environment: High-resolution desktop monitor

Goal: PJ wants a general dashboard alongside the slot machine to consistently display his stats. He wants to be able to see the history of his wins and his betting stats.
Additionally, he wants to make use of his very expensive monitor with high-fidelity neon animations. 

Frustrations: PJ is a man of little patience and time. He wants the ability to "skip animation" when desired as well as high-performance rendering so the spin reels don't lag.
He also wants shortcuts for his keyboard like being able to use the spacebar to spin or the arrow keys to adjust his bet amount.
