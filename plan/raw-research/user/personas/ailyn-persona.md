Persona : Minerva Minimal

Age : 22

Occupation : Writer

Tech environment : iPhone SE

Goal : Minerva is an avid follower of minimalism but sometimes still misses the feeling of having large material winnings such as money from slot-machine games. However she still wants to keep minimal physical belongings and keep her space and mine clear.

Frustrations : Minerva has only a basic and small cellphone and can feel overwhelmed by noisy sound features. She would really appreciate it if there was a way to turn off sounds. Moreover since her phone has a small screen she would like to have simple but intuitive UI that not only adapts to her small phone but also her relatively simple lifestyle.
