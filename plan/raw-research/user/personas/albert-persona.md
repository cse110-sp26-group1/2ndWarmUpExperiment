Persona: Joe Simon

Age: 29

Occupation: UX Designer at a gaming startup

Tech Environment: Dual-monitor desktop setup with a mechanical keyboard and ultrawide curved display

Goal: Joe wants a visually polished slot machine experience that feels immersive without being confusing. He wants a clean side panel that shows bankroll, current bet, win streaks, recent outcomes, and bonus progress at a glance. He also enjoys sleek lighting effects, smooth reel motion, and satisfying sound/visual feedback that make each spin feel premium.

Frustrations: Joe gets annoyed when flashy effects interfere with usability. He wants the interface to stay readable during intense animations and expects responsive controls with no input delay. He also wants keyboard shortcuts like spacebar to spin, up/down arrows to change bet size, and a quick toggle to reduce motion or skip long bonus animations when he is playing faster.
