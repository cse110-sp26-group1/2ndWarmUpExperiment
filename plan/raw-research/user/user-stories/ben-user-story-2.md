# User: Sros (Gambler)

This user wants the slot machine to feel exciting and highly rewarding, especially when hitting big wins.

They expect strong visual and audio feedback when a large win occurs, such as flashing lights, coin animations, or particle effects. The experience should create a sense of excitement and keep them engaged for longer sessions.

The system should support different levels of rewards (small win vs big win) with different intensities of feedback. Larger wins should feel significantly more impactful than smaller ones to encourage continued play.

The goal is to maximize engagement through rewarding feedback and immersive effects. (Layered animations, particle systems, dynamic audio feedback based on win size)
