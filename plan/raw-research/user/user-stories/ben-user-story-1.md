# User: Marcus (Casual Player)

Marcus wants a fast and simple slot machine experience that feels smooth and easy to use.

He expects to open the app and immediately press a spin button without navigating menus. The reels should spin quickly and smoothly, and the results should be clear and easy to understand. If the experience feels slow or confusing, he will stop playing.

The system should allow users to repeatedly spin with minimal delay and no extra steps. The focus is on speed, simplicity, and responsiveness. (Fast UI response, minimal interaction steps, efficient animation rendering)
