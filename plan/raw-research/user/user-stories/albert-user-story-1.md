User: Marcus (frequent slot player)

User Story:
Marcus likes fast-paced gameplay and wants the slot machine to feel exciting and responsive. He wants a clear way to spin quickly, track his balance and bet amount, and get a strong visual reward when he lands a big win. This helps him stay engaged without getting confused by the interface.(Keyboard input handling, UI state updates, CSS/Canvas animations, browser audio API)


- The player can press the spacebar or click a Spin button to start a spin.
- The current balance and bet amount are always visible on screen.
- When the player gets a big win, the game shows a celebratory animation such as coins,  glow effects, or screen highlights.
- A win sound plays during a big win event.
- The game updates the balance immediately after the spin result is determined.
- The interface remains smooth and usable while animations are playing.
