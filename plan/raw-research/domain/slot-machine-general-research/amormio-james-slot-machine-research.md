## PATTERNS (Game Mechanics)

### Megaways Engine:

A lot of modern slots are based on the Megaways engine.

- Added multiple rows for each spin (increased volatility, increased player retention).
- Also provides the possibility of much higher payouts (sometimes up to 100,000x while increasing the # of ways to win).

### Cluster Pays:

Match adjacent symbols anywhere. Simplifies player understanding, rewards dense symbol groups.

### Cascading Reels:

Winning symbols disappear → new symbols fall in (like Tetris). Increases engagement, extends gameplay, adds chain-reaction excitement.

### Free Spins:

- Free spins with scatter symbols leading to more free spins on top of a bonus multiplier.
- Free Spins (less RTP), Cutscenes, Free Prizes, mini-games, multipliers, etc.

### Bonus Rounds:

Bonus Rounds (90s) are a cheap way to improve player retention.

- Watch a short story potentially leading to a prize.
- Or choose the correct item (e.g. treasure chest) with the prize (2 possible outcomes)

1. All revealed at the end = player has free will.
2. Reveals player choices only (and not unpicked) = predetermined.

### Themes:

- Themes (Egypt, Space, movie/game franchises...) increase engagement, player becomes invested.
- Game elements (achievements, leaderboards, constant sound effects...) pair with thematic storytelling, narrative players can follow.

## HOW TO IMPROVE PLAYER RETENTION

- Hourly Jackpots, Bonus Games, Daily Drops, etc.
- Accessibility (24/7 Support, Simple Navigation, UX Consistency, Accessible Designs)
- Building a Lifestyle Product (Personalization, Constant Updates)
- Userbase Focused Design (Testing of Users, Mobile-First Design)

### META GAMES & TOURNAMENTS

- Meta Games are popular to add extra player incentives
- Quickspin Tournaments (getting points based off events, playing against other players)
- Casino Races (players compete like in QuickSpin Tournaments, winners get prizes)

## SLOT MACHINE HISTORY

### 1880s:

Origins Originating from the 1880s (originally based on Poker hands). No direct payout originally (free beers, cigarettes, etc.). Using just the suits/symbols most often.

### Late 1890s:

Simplification & Real Money In the late 1890s machines moved away from Poker to simplify (also started to give out real money).

### 1960s:

Electronic Era In the 1960s, the first fully electronic slot machine was made. Attendantless Payout and a bottomless hopper for the first time.

### 1970s:

Video Slots In the 1970s the first video slot machine was developed in Las Vegas.

### 1990s:

Online Gambling First online slot machine was made by Microgaming in the 1990s.

### Late 2000s-2010s:

Mobile & Franchises Rise of Mobile Slots and Franchise Themes. Freemium models (free with in-app purchases for advantages).

#3# 2010s onwards:
Gamification Level-ups, achievements, daily quests, leaderboards.
